# 💼 The Business Owner's Prompts for Claude — Part 1: Core Business

> **How to use this guide:** Copy any prompt, replace the `[bracketed]` placeholders with your details, and paste it into Claude (free or paid).
> - **Fill-in-the-blank prompts** produce structured, ready-to-use output.
> - **Question-based prompts** help you explore and go deeper.
>
> *This is Part 1 of the Business Owner's guide (Core Business). Further parts cover Customer Service & Sales, Real Estate & Property Management, Lead Generation & Nurturing, HR, and Support.*
