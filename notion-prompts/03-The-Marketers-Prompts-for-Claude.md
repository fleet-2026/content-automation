# 📣 The Marketer's Prompts for Claude

> **How to use this guide:** Copy any prompt, replace the `[bracketed]` placeholders with your details, and paste it into Claude (free or paid).
> - **Fill-in-the-blank prompts** produce structured, ready-to-use output.
> - **Question-based prompts** help you explore and go deeper.

---

## 🧰 General Business Toolkit

### 💥 Researching Your Competitors

**FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS:**

- Help me analyze my competitors in the [industry] space. My main competitors are [list]. Compare their positioning, offers, and messaging.
- Create a competitor analysis framework for [niche], covering strengths, weaknesses, and gaps I could exploit.

**QUESTIONS-BASED PROMPTS:**

- "What's the best way to research competitors in my industry?"

**EXAMPLES:**

- *Filled-in example:* "Help me analyze my competitors in the online fitness coaching space. My main competitors are [list]. Compare their positioning, offers, and messaging."
