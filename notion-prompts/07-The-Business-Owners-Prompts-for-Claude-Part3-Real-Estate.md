# 💼 The Business Owner's Prompts for Claude — Part 3: Real Estate

> **How to use this guide:** Copy a prompt, replace the `[bracketed]` placeholders, and paste into Claude.
> - **Fill-in-the-blank prompts** give structured output. **Question-based prompts** help you explore.
>
> *Part 3 of the Business Owner's guide.*

## 🏠 Real Estate

### 📄 Generate Real Estate Flyers

**FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS:**

- Write flyer copy for a [property type] at [address/area] priced at [price], highlighting [key features].
- Create an eye-catching open-house flyer for [property].
- Draft flyer text for a [rental/sale] listing targeting [buyer/renter type].

**QUESTIONS-BASED PROMPTS:**

- "What makes a real estate flyer effective?"
- "What information must a property flyer include?"
- "How do I make a flyer visually appealing?"
- "How do I write copy that drives showings?"
- "How do I tailor a flyer to the target buyer?"

**EXAMPLES:**

- *Filled-in example:* "Write flyer copy for a 3-bed family home in a quiet suburb priced at $450,000, highlighting the large backyard and updated kitchen."
