# 💼 The Business Owner's Prompts for Claude — Part 9: Human Resources (A) — Recruiting, Onboarding, Performance & Development

> **How to use this guide:** Copy a prompt, replace the `[bracketed]` placeholders, and paste into Claude.
> - **Fill-in-the-blank prompts** give structured output. **Question-based prompts** help you explore.
>
> *Part 9 of the Business Owner's guide. HR is split across two files (A: people-facing; B: workforce, HR tech, data & compliance).*
> *Note: HR documents and policies have legal implications that vary by jurisdiction — have qualified HR/legal professionals review anything binding.*

**FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS:**
